There's nothing more musical than a sunset. A time when daylight fades and the sun is no longer visible. It's not the most extravagant definition to represent all the beauty the sky beholds.

The glamour lies within the effervescent sky during a sunset. It combines the serenity of the auburn sun, pale pink hues, and the rich light radiating from the ends of the sky to the last grain of sand in the ocean to achieve absolute perfection. While sunset are a literal end to a day, they've also become symbol of peace, harmony and the promise of renewal.

We are like islands in the sea, separate on the surface but connected on the deep. There's no denying it. Thinking about and dreaming of the ocean brings us both joy and calmness and truly soothes the soul. Sea is a storehouse of treasure in the form of uncountable life forms of which a lot are yet to be discovered.

Sea is a very mysterious place and due to its vast nature, it always drive human being curious about it. The heart of man is very much like the sea, it has its storms, it has its tides and in its depths it has its pearls too.

Nothing better than sun, sand and the sunkissed sea. I wonder how long this rapture will last, for how long sun unpin sea it's spell will cast and soon might turn crimson into embrace tight. When sun will love sea away from everyone's sight and sea will melt in sun's shadow surpassed. Oh blushing sea, turning into blood wine when sun kissed sea on it's lips, fear of divorce that morning might bring.

Sun hear now the calls of the wind, wake up on crimson sea like tender hind and dawning sun kiss sea so divine yet unconscious sea blushes and turn into blood wine
